### Trade-offs (Accepted Design — Not Blockers)

**LDR-04 — Leaderboard update latency (0–7 seconds):**
`balance:update` socket event updates `balanceStore` only; `leaderboardStore` refreshes via 7-second broadcast timer only. Within the approved design spec ("approximately every 5-10 seconds" per locked CONTEXT.md decision). No requirement violation.

**ANTI-03 — GET game routes not rate-limited:**
`gameLimiter` and `clickInterval` applied to all 9 POST game bet routes. GET `/games/mines/active-session` and GET `/games/blackjack/active-session` (session restore on reconnect) have no rate limiting. These endpoints have no balance mutations and are inherently lower-risk than bet-placement routes. Accepted design.
